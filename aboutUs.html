About Us

The city of Jalandhar aims to be the leading sports and manufacturing hub in Asia. This identity with sports is further extended to its citizens to makethem active and healthy. The city sets for itself 7 goals. The city aims to be inclusive by reinvigorating city spaces, promote sports by increasing sporting venues, encouraging training and education in sports. Further city wants to expand and diversify the manufacturing based economy and simultaneously transform from production to education, research and innovation at the same time, improve walk ability and public mobility. .
